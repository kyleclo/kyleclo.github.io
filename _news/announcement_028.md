Two new preprints! [Olmix](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.12237) is the data mixing framework we used during Olmo 3, addressing real challenges we resolve when taking data mixing from research literature to actual practice. [How2Everything](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.08808) mines thousands of real-world "how to do X" instructions from the web and synthesizes them into a dataset, training procedure, and eval suite. Congrats to Mayee and Yapei!
